a little bit about me

Hello! I'm KyLee Cameron, a graphic design student at Utah Valley University, born and raised in small town Nevada. Art has been a passion of mine since childhood that I came to rediscover a few years ago. I decided that I wanted to take my pursuit of art more seriously in college and focus on applied art, particularly graphic design. I'm passionate about finding innovative ways to express myself and visually communicate ideas and messages through design.
